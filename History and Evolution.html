Our history and evolution

The evolution of ALTAR INDUSTRIES

  • 2032 - Our founder, Aidan Innocks, begins working at IdeaHubb.
  • 2035 - Our incredible founder discovers an idea for VR holidays through his discussions with IdeaHubb's AI system.
  • 2040 - After 5 years of assidious hard work, grueling litigation, and securing the necessary funding, ALTAR INDUSTRIES is finally announced to the public.
  • 2042 - Our first Venture 'ISLAND: the remote luxury Venture' is released, to much public excitement. ISLAND remains one of our most popular ventures to date!
  • 2043 - Throughout 2043, two more Ventures are released; 'SAFARI: An African safari Venture' and 'SOUTH: An Antartic Venture'.
  • 2044 - The 2nd Venture tech is released, making Ventures considerably more incricate.
  • 2046 - We release 3rd Venture, which enabled venturers to interact with others in the same Venture. 'RETRO: A Venture through the 80s' is also released.
  • 2047 - ALTAR INDUSTRIES announces a major restructure, sparking huge company-wide change, and expanding our offerings to above and beyond. These changes made the ALTAR INDUSTRIES that you know and love today!
  • 2050 - 'WEST: A wildwest Venture' and 'PROHIBITION: A 1920s Venture' are released alongside 4th Venture to celebrate our 10th anniversary.
  • 2052 - For it's 10th anniversary, the new, updated ISLAND is revealed. The Venture 'BERMUDA: A Bermuda Triangle Venture' is also released.
  • 2055 - Our first off earth Venture, 'LUNAR: Venture to the moon', is released alongside 4th Venture for our 15th anniversary.
  • 2057 - The Venture 'VICTORIA: A Victorian-era Venture' is released.
  • 2060 - 5 years after the first, our second off earth Venture 'MARS: a martian Venture' is released, for our 25th anniversary.
  • 2062 - Our Venture 'BERMUDA: A Bermuda Triangle Venture' is released. Our much loved Venture South is retired, to make way for new and greater Ventures!
  • 2063 (Current) - We have just announced the planned updates for our ventures LUNAR and PROHIBITION, to coincide with the launch of 5th Venture. We also sadly announced that we will be retiring the Ventures SAFARI and Retro in 2065, to make way for our two new and greater Ventures!
  • 2065 - Our two up and coming Ventures, 'SATURN: aboard the Starline' and 'INSIDE OUR EARTH: A Jules Verne Venture' will be released!
